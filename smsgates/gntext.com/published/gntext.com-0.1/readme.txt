Module: SMS gateway for gntext.com
Author: Gennady Telegin <support@itxd.ru>

To install copy contents of 'upload' directory to the root of your server.

In shop settings under SMS tab fill next parameters:
1. Choose 'gntext' sms gatewey
2. Enter Login - Your unique account identifier found online in the Admin Panel as your account Client_Id
3. Enter Password - Your password found in the Admin Panel of your online account as Client_Pass
4. Enter Sender ID - An 11-character string or phone number that shows the recipient who sent the SMS
